📸 Overview
CollegeMate is a lightweight web app designed to help college students stay organized and on top of their academic life — all from a single, beautiful dashboard.
Whether it's tracking your attendance before it drops below 75%, managing assignment deadlines, or keeping notes handy — CollegeMate has you covered. 💪

✨ Features
📅 Timetable Manager

Add subjects with their scheduled time
View your full weekly class schedule at a glance
Delete entries with a single click

📊 Attendance Tracker

Enter total and attended classes to calculate your attendance percentage
Instant status — Safe ✅ or Shortage ⚠️ (75% threshold)
Full attendance history saved with date logs

📝 Assignment Manager

Add assignments with deadlines
Never miss a submission again
Clean list view with delete support

✅ To-Do / Notes

Quick notes and task tracking
Click to mark tasks as done (strikethrough style)
Persistent across sessions


💾 Data Storage
All your data is saved using localStorage — meaning:

✅ No account needed
✅ Works completely offline
✅ Data stays even after closing the browser
❌ Clears if browser data/cache is wiped
